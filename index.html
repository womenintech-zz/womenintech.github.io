Women in Tech Resources

The pipeline problem

Women fill close to half of all jobs in the U.S. economy[1], yet only 20% of programmers are women.[2] Statistics of 10% or less female technical workforce are not uncommon.[3] Furthermore, the percentage of women in technical careers has decreased since the 1980s.[4]

Given the assumption that there is a problem here to fix, how do we uncover the problem, and how do we fix it?
